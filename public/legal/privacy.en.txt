1. GENERAL PROVISIONS
1.1. This Privacy Policy (the "Policy") defines the procedure for collection, use, storage, and protection of information obtained by the Limited Liability Partnership "GRT Company" (the "Company", the "Operator") from users of the website https://future-book.org (the "Platform", the "Site", the "Service").
1.2. Use of the Platform means the User’s unconditional agreement with this Policy and the terms of information processing.
1.3. If the User does not agree with the Policy, the User must stop using the Platform.
1.4. The Policy is developed in accordance with the laws of the Republic of Kazakhstan, including rules on information and personal data protection.

2. COMPANY PRINCIPLES
2.1. The Company does not require or request personal data that directly identify an individual.
2.2. The Service is designed so that the User:
- does not provide full name, IIN, residential address, passport data;
- does not enter real people’s names;
- uses anonymized and generalized information.
2.3. The User is responsible for keeping entered data anonymized.

3. WHAT INFORMATION MAY BE PROCESSED
3.1. Within the Platform, the Company may process the following information:
A) technical information automatically transmitted when using the Site:
- IP address;
- browser and device type;
- cookie data;
- date and time of access;
- session technical parameters;
B) anonymized user information voluntarily entered by the User:
- generalized facts about life (without personal data);
- formulations of desires, goals, and expectations;
- text prompts for generating a fictional work;
C) limited payment information:
- payment identifier;
- amount and payment date;
- transaction status.
The Company does not store or process full bank card data — such data is processed exclusively by payment partners.

4. PURPOSES OF INFORMATION PROCESSING
4.1. Information is processed solely for the following purposes:
- providing access to the Service;
- forming and providing the Result (book);
- ensuring technical operation of the Platform;
- processing payments and confirming payment;
- improving service quality;
- preventing abuse and violations of the offer terms.
4.2. The Company does not use information for:
- identifying the User;
- transferring data to third parties for commercial purposes;
- sending advertising without the User’s consent.

5. COOKIES AND ANALYTICS
5.1. The Platform may use cookies and similar technologies to:
- ensure proper Site operation;
- analyze traffic;
- improve the user experience.
5.2. The User may restrict or disable cookies in browser settings; however, some Platform features may not work correctly.

6. TRANSFER OF INFORMATION TO THIRD PARTIES
6.1. The Company does not transfer User information to third parties except:
- to payment partners — only to the extent required to process payments;

6.2. Transfer of information is carried out with confidentiality and security measures.

7.1. The Company takes reasonable organizational and technical measures to protect information from:

7.2. Information retention periods are determined by processing purposes and the laws of the Republic of Kazakhstan.
